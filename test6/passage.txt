How to Treat Disabled People
In our daily life, there are many people, who are disabled physically and mentally. They are likely to have experienced some severe troubles or accidents, and they hope us, normal people, not to see them as the disabled. Therefore, we require mastering some basic tips to treat disabled people correctly and appropriately.

Above all, we require showing respect for them, and we cannot laugh at them. Thanks to their disability, they may consider themselves as inferior persons. Therefore, enough respect for them is of great importance for the disabled.

Additionally, we are supposed to help the disabled out when they face some troubles which they may not solve due to handicaps caused physically or mentally.

Last but not least, they require constant encouragement from different kinds of people to keep themselves leading a more confident life. So what we need to do more essentially is to encourage them.
